An experimental, simple, yet feature-rich to do list

Demo

To Do is written in HTML, PHP, CSS, and JS/jQuery.
Note: To Do is experimental software. As such, it may not be appropriate for all situations.

To Do is somewhat unique in that it doesn’t rely on a database for keeping track of list items. As a result, it is more lightweight than database-reliant to do lists. Almost everything is handled by jQuery and AJAX; PHP only handles the saving of the list and (optional) password protection. The password protection is extremely simple so To Do isn’t recommended for situations involving sensitive data.
